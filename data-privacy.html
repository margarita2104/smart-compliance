Data Privacy Policy

We are using cookies and tracking services in order to display and improve our website technically for you and to provide content that is relevant to your interests, we use tracking services based on cookies or equivalent functions. We also embed content from Google, such as YouTube videos and Google Maps. Your data will also be transferred to the USA. The US is an unsafe third country. By confirming the «Accept all» button, you agree to the above described processing of your data by cookies and additionally the transfer of your data to third countries. By clicking the «Show details» button, you can choose which category of cookies you want to allow or reject all and find more detail. For further information, please refer also to our Data protection and privacy policy.

We are using cookies and tracking services You seem surprisingly interested in our consent management. Of course, we are pleased about this; nevertheless, we must admit that we are also concerned about you. You can find help on this topic on our numerous articles, podcasts or webinars or simply contact us. You are not alone!

Necessary cookies help to technically display the website by enabling basic functions such as page navigation and ensuring the operation of the website. The website cannot function properly without these cookies, therefore we use these cookies without your consent. You can find more information in our Privacy Policy.

Statistics services help us to analyse user behaviour on our website and thus improve our website. We use Google Analytics for this purpose. You can learn more about this in our Privacy Policy.

Third party content We embed content from third parties. This means that our website offers maps from Google Maps and videos from YouTube. YouTube videos are embedded via the data protection-friendly mode, provided you have given your consent. This means that only when you click on a video data such as your IP address will be transmitted. Your data will be transmitted to the USA. The US is an unsafe third country, which means there is no level of data protection comparable to that in the EU. There is no adequacy decision for the US by the EU Commission. In the USA, there is a risk that state authorities will gain access to your data. However, as only pseudonyms are transferred, it is not possible to draw conclusions about your person. If you agree to the integration of YouTube videos and Google Maps, you also consent to the transfer of your data to third countries. You can find more information on the transfer of data to third countries and concerning the use of YouTube and Google Maps in our Privacy Policy.

Log files

When you visit our website, information is automatically sent to our website server by the browser used on your terminal device. This information is temporarily stored in a so-called log file. The following information is collected without your intervention and stored until automatic deletion:

— IP address of the requesting computer,

— Date and time of access,

— name and URL of the file accessed,

— website from which the access was made (referrer URL),

— browser used and, if applicable, the operating system of your computer as well as the name of your access provider.

This data is collected and processed in order to ensure the use of our website, namely the establishment of the connection as well as system security and stability in the long term and to optimise our internet offer and evaluate it for internal statistical purposes.

Within the scope of application of the GDPR, we invoke the legal basis of legitimate interest within the meaning of Art. 6 (1) lit. f GDPR in this regard.

An IP address can be personal. However, this will only be analysed in the event of an attack on the network infrastructure of our website or if there is suspicion of unauthorised or improper use of the website for investigation purposes or for defence in connection with any lawful claims or, if necessary, used for the purpose of identification in criminal proceedings and in the context of civil and criminal proceedings. The legal basis for processing for these purposes within the scope of the GDPR are our legitimate interests within the meaning of Art. 6 (1) lit. f GDPR.

Tracking tools

We use Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google Ireland Limited, Google Building Gordon House, 4 Barrow St, Dublin, D04 E5W5, Ireland or Google Inc, 1600 Amphitheatre Pkwy, Mountain View, CA 94043-1351, USA. Google Analytics uses methods that enable an analysis of the use of the website, such as cookies. These generate information about your use of this website, such as:

This data is transferred to and stored on Google servers located in the U.S. By activating IP anonymisation («anonymiseIP») on this website, your IP address is shortened before transmission within the member states of the European Union or other states party to the Agreement on the European Economic Area and Switzerland.

According to Google, the anonymised IP address transmitted by the user’s browser as part of Google Analytics will not be merged with other Google data. Only in exceptional cases can the full IP address be transmitted to a Google server in the USA and shortened there. In these cases, we ensure through contractual guarantees that Google maintains a sufficient level of data protection. The information will be used to evaluate the use of the website, to compile reports on website activity and to provide other services relating to website and internet usage for the purposes of market research and website customisation. Google may also transfer this information to third parties where required to do so by law, or where such third parties process the information on Google’s behalf. According to Google, a connection between the IP address and other user data will not be established under any circumstances.

Within the scope of application of the GDPR, this processing is carried out for our legitimate interest within the meaning of Art. 6 (1) lit. f GDPR. You can object to this data processing at any time by notifying us. You can also prevent the data collection described above by downloading and installing the browser plug-in available at the following link: http://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout?hl=en

An opt-out cookie will be stored on your device. If you delete cookies, the link must be clicked again.

We use Google Maps API (Application Programming Interface, «Google Maps») from Google Ireland Limited, Gordon House, Barrow Street, Dublin 4, Ireland, or Google Inc., 1600 Amphitheatre Pkwy, Mountain View, CA 94043-1351, USA, on our website for the visual display of geographical information (maps). By using Google Maps, information about the use of our website, including your IP address, is transmitted to a Google server in the USA and stored there.

Within the scope of application of the GDPR, the legal basis for processing data for this purpose is our legitimate interest pursuant to Art. 6 (1) lit. f GDPR. You can object to this data processing at any time by notifying us.

It is possible to deactivate Google Maps and prevent data transfers to Google by deactivating JavaScript in your browser. This will result in you not being able to use the map display.

Data security

The security of your data is important to us. We use the widespread SSL (Secure Socket Layer) procedure in conjunction with the highest level of encryption supported by your browser when you visit our website. You can tell whether an individual page of our website is encrypted by the closed display of the key or lock symbol in the lower status bar of your browser.

We also use appropriate technical and organisational security measures to protect your data against accidental or intentional manipulation, partial or complete loss, destruction or against unauthorised access by SMART-compliance GmbH employees or third parties (need-to-know access). Our security measures are continuously improved in line with technological developments.
