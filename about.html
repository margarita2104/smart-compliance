About us

Meet the Founder

Ekaterina Anthony, the founder of SMART-compliance GmbH, provides compliance support to fintech start-ups.

Ekaterina has served over 20 years in various roles within the exchanges, fin-tech, and commodity trading. Based out of London, she was head of the Moscow Stock Exchange office in the UK for the global business operation, cross border compliance and clients’ onboarding. Also served as head of representation for Eastern Europe for the commodity valuation firm S&P Global Platts. Before founding her company, she worked for 2,5 years at gwp in crypto/DLT sector compliance.

The most recent work focused on the development of regulatory and compliance frameworks that address blockchain & crypto projects management. Ekaterina holds an MBA in finance from California State University and DAS in Compliance Management from Hochschule Luzern. She also acts as a Board Member of Swiss Crypto Valley Association and Chairwoman of IDAXA.
