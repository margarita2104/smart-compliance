SMART-compliance partners with CRYPTO2030 in Davos

SMART-compliance GmbH is proud to announce our partnership with CRYPTO2030, taking place in Davos, Switzerland from January 15th to 19th, 2024. As a leading company in Switzerland, specializing in helping entrepreneurs obtain the necessary permits to run their businesses, we are excited to join forces with CRYPTO2030 in advancing the future of Web3.

CRYPTO2030 in Davos is a landmark event for the blockchain and crypto community, bringing together innovators, entrepreneurs, and thought leaders from around the world to UNITE, INNOVATE, and TRANSFORM the landscape of Web3 technologies. With a lineup of distinguished speakers and industry experts, this event promises to be a gathering of immense value.

Ekaterina Anthony, CEO and Board Member of SMART-compliance GmbH, will be a featured speaker and moderator at the event, sharing insights and expertise on regulatory compliance and its critical role in the blockchain and crypto industry.
